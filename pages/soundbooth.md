The Studio sits in a very high-traffic area on the first floor of U of I Library. More importantly, it is sandwiched between the Circulation Desk and our Special Collections reading room. Knowing that musicians would undoubtedly be a big part of our user base, we decided to invest in a soundbooth. While not 100% sound proof, it does a great job of dampening the excess noise and also elevates the space! We ended up with an Enhanced WhisperRoom package for even more noise reduction, as well as a few other add-ons, which you can explore below!
